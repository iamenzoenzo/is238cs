PLEMA FAQ

Philippine Local Emergency App
Providing emergency services communication to every Juan and Juana.

Step 1:
Text INFO to
21581493
(Cross-telco: 225651493)

Step 2: To subscribe
Reply YES

Step 3: To get available keywords
Text PLEMA-HELP

Step 4: To get all emergency phone numbers in that City:
Text PLEMA-GET <space> <CITY CODE>

To get the specific number for the agency in the city:
Text PLEMA-GET <space> <CITY CODE> <space> <AGENCY CODE>

List of Available City Codes

QC, NCR, MNL, BGC

List of Available Agency Codes

BANTAYBATA, EME, MANILAWATE, MAYNILAD, MERALCO, MMDA, PGH, PNP, REDCROSS, RESQ, STLUKES
